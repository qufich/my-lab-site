The Theoretical Chemical Physics (TCP) group at the University of Luxembourg is looking for motivated researchers to join our team. We work on developing novel methods and understanding complex systems at the intersection of physics, chemistry, and materials science.

We value creativity, curiosity, and a collaborative spirit. Our interdisciplinary research spans quantum mechanics, machine learning, molecular dynamics, and more.

PhD Positions

We regularly have openings for PhD students in various research areas. Candidates should have a strong background in physics, chemistry, or related fields, with good programming skills and interest in theoretical/computational work.

Postdoctoral Positions

We welcome applications from postdoctoral researchers interested in our research areas. Candidates should have a PhD in physics, chemistry, or related fields, with relevant research experience and publication record.
